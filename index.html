About RIVAJ

Serving Tradition on a Platter

Welcome to RIVAJ, your home for authentic Gujarati cuisine in the heart of Gandhinagar. Our name, meaning 'tradition', reflects our commitment to preserving the rich culinary heritage of Gujarat.

We believe in the joy of a shared meal. Our daily-changing thali is thoughtfully prepared with the freshest seasonal ingredients, offering a wholesome and delightful experience that brings families and friends together.

Our Thali

The RIVAJ Experience

Our thali is a celebration of authentic Gujarati cuisine, offering a perfect balance of sweet, spicy, and savory flavors. The menu changes daily based on the freshest seasonal produce.

What's in Our Thali?

Farsan (Savory Snacks)

Delicious steamed or fried snacks to start your meal.

Shaak (Vegetable Curries)

A variety of seasonal vegetable preparations.

Kathol (Lentils/Pulses)

A wholesome and protein-rich pulse dish.

Dal or Kadhi

A comforting sweet and sour lentil soup or yogurt-based curry.

Roti / Puri & Rice

Soft flatbreads or fluffy fried bread, served with steamed rice.

Mithai (Sweet Dish)

A delightful traditional sweet to complete your meal.
